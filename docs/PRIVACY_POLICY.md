# Privacy Policy — AI PII Guard (Local)

AI PII Guard is designed to keep your text local.

## Data collection
AI PII Guard does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Network access
AI PII Guard makes **no network requests**.

## What the extension can access
### Popup scrubber
When you paste text into the extension popup, it scrubs the text locally on your device.

### Paste Guard (optional)
If you enable Paste Guard, the extension runs on supported AI chat sites and listens for **paste** events in text inputs.

- In *Calm* mode, it shows a local confirmation dialog and asks whether to paste the original or scrubbed text.
- In *Force* mode, it auto-replaces pasted text with scrubbed text (you can undo with Ctrl+Z).

The extension does not send pasted content anywhere.

## Storage
The extension stores only your settings (mode and which detectors are enabled) using Chrome’s extension storage.
